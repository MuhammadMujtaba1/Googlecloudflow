# FlowThread Demo Video Script (30 Seconds)

**Goal:** Highlight the transition from a fragmented workflow to a unified one.

---

### **[0:00 - 0:05] The Problem**
**Visual:** Fast-paced montage of a freelancer's screen. Switching between Slack (chatting), Trello (tasks), Stripe (invoicing), Gmail (delivery), and Google Drive (files).
**Audio (SFX):** Rapid mouse clicking, notification sounds from different apps.
**Voiceover:** "Still juggling five different apps just to finish one project?"

### **[0:05 - 0:10] The Solution**
**Visual:** Screen clears. A single, clean window opens: **FlowThread**.
**Audio (Music):** Calm, upbeat, rhythmic lo-fi beat starts.
**Voiceover:** "Meet FlowThread. Where your conversation *is* your workspace."

### **[0:10 - 0:20] The Magic Moment**
**Visual:** 
1. A chat message: "Can you design the logo by Friday?" 
2. User clicks a button next to the message.
3. A task card instantly appears in the sidebar: "Logo Design - $200 - Friday".
**Voiceover:** "Turn chat points into tasks instantly. No more manual entry."

### **[0:20 - 0:25] Delivery & Payment**
**Visual:** 
1. Freelancer clicks "Deliver". 
2. Client clicks "Pay". 
3. A "Payment Successful" checkmark appears.
**Voiceover:** "Deliver work and get paid securely, all in one thread."

### **[0:25 - 0:30] Call to Action**
**Visual:** FlowThread logo + "Join the Beta" + URL.
**Voiceover:** "FlowThread. Work smarter, not harder. Join the beta today."

---

## **Production Notes:**
- **Style:** Clean, minimalist UI recording with smooth transitions.
- **Color Palette:** Indigo, Slate, and White.
- **Tone:** Professional, efficient, and modern.
